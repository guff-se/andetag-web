# Music from ANDETAG

Music is a central part of ANDETAG. A slow rhythm. A shared breath.

Here you find the album heard in the exhibition in Stockholm. Twelve tracks. Just over an hour of continuous listening. Music made to inhabit a space, rather than fill it.

The compositions are created by Gustaf Tadaa and [**Povel Olsson**](https://povelolsson.com/). The sound moves between piano and flute, electronic textures, and quiet field recordings. A passing train. Wind in the trees outside the house where the music took shape. The everyday, listened to slowly.

Each track unfolds as its own story. A movement between approach and resistance. Tension and release. Not to lead somewhere else, but to remain with what is.

The music is made for listening. At home. In headphones. In a room where time is allowed to slow down.

ANDETAG is available on [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/album/16JNuKeBQUK3RZHQjj4nDV?si=nWPhkTpmRKKTFDhi3GBQsQ), [Apple Music](https://music.apple.com/se/album/andetag/1843599465), [Soundcloud](https://soundcloud.com/andetag) och [Bandcamp](https://andetagmuseum.bandcamp.com/).
