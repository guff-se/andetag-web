## Give ANDETAG

A gift does not have to be an object.  
It can be a moment.

ANDETAG is an immersive art experience where light, textile and music move in rhythm with a shared breath. A quiet place inside the city. A pause from pace and input.

To give ANDETAG is to invite someone into presence.  
To step out of motion, for a while.

### Two ways to give

**Gift card**  
An open invitation. The recipient chooses when to attend. Delivered digitally and valid for all public sessions in Stockholm.

[ Buy Gift Card ](https://andetag.understory.io/en/giftcards)

**Ticket as a gift**  
A specific moment in time.  
You choose the date and give the experience directly.

[ Book ticket ](/en/stockholm/tickets/#book)

#### When ANDETAG is given

Often to someone who already has enough.  
To someone drawn to art, light and music.  
To someone in need of a pause.

Sometimes for a birthday.  
Sometimes for no reason at all.

### Praktisk information

Location: Central Stockholm, Hötorget

Duration: Approximately one hour

Limited number of guests per session
