### Visitor reviews of ANDETAG in Stockholm

ANDETAG is an art experience in central Stockholm, frequently reviewed by both local and international visitors.  
Below is a curated selection of excerpts from Tripadvisor reviews.

**Overview**

  * 165 total reviews

  * 152 five-star ratings

  * Average rating: 4.9 / 5

  * Top ranked art museum in Stockholm on TripAdvisor

### Why visitors recommend ANDETAG

Visitors frequently highlight:

  * Calm atmosphere and slow pace

  * Immersive light and textile art

  * Central Stockholm location

  * Flexible visit length
