## Tickets

ANDETAGis a quiet art experience in central Stockholm.  
A place to step out of the pace of everyday life and take a shared breath.

Timed booking is used to maintain a calm flow of visitors, ensuring that too many people do not arrive at the same time.

You are welcome to arrive before your booked time, as long as [regular opening hours](https://www.andetag.museum/en/stockholm/opening-hours/) apply. Tickets can also be purchased on site, subject to availability.

At selected times, Art Yoga is offered, a gentle yoga class that can also be booked through the booking calendar.

### Prices

**Regular ticket:** 240 SEK (190 SEK daytime)

**Student/senior:** 200 SEK (150 SEK daytime)

**Youth (8–17 years):** 125 SEK

**Art Yoga**(Tuesdays 17.00-18.00)**:** 320 kr

Daytime pricing: weekdays 12.00 – 17.00
