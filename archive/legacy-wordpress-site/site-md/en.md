# The Breathing Art Museum in Stockholm

Inside ANDETAG, time stands still.

ANDETAG is an immersive art experience in central Stockholm, where light, breath, and music meet in a quiet museum near Hötorget.

In a world where the pace is constantly accelerating, You are invited to slow your breath and be enveloped in an enchanting tapestry of shapes, light, and sound.

Welcome to ANDETAG – a place of stillness and wonder.

What is ANDETAG? 

ANDETAG is a breathing art museum and experience in Stockholm. Visitors enter a room of light and woven optical textiles that respond to breath and sound. The experience blends contemporary art, meditation, and stillness, and has become an appreciated alternative for art lovers, curious visitors, and anyone seeking a quiet pause in the middle of the city.

You may stay as long as you want. The average visitor stays around 50 minutes.

Is ANDETAGsuitable for children? 

Yes, we’ve had many kids visit us an belive that all ages can find a calmer rhythm at ANDETAG.

Please note that there is no space for strollers inside the venue. You can lock your stroller at the bike parking on Sveavägen.

Can I buy tickets on-site? 

Yes, you can pay at the door as long as there is space available.

Can I cancel or reschedule my ticket? 

You can cancel your visit up until your scheduled time. Refunds are issued as a gift card, which can be used to book a new time.

A cancellation link is included in your confirmation email.

What happens if I miss my scheduled time? 

If you are less than 30 minutes late, you are warmly welcome. After that, we may be able to accommodate you if space allows.

Is the experience accessible? 

There are elevators to both street level and the subway. The exhibition is wheelchair accessible, but unfortunately, we do not have a fully equipped wheelchair-accessible restroom.

Some parts of the exhibition are dark, but navigation is relatively straightforward. Guide dogs are welcome.

There is no hearing loop available, but the music can be accessed online if you’d like to play it on your own device.

Caregivers or assistants are admitted free of charge.

## Tickets

ANDETAGis an experience that takes you from the stress of everyday life into a peaceful, magical world.

You are welcome to before your scheduled time, providing we are open normally. Explore the exhibition at your own pace and stay until you feel ready to leave.

We use time slots to ensure that too many people don’t arrive at the same time. Spots fill up quickly, so make sure to book in advance.

## Art Yoga - Every Tuesday at 17.00

Art Yoga is a slow yoga class held inside Andetag’s immersive art environment in central Stockholm.  
A place to move, breathe, and settle, in the middle of the city.

Surrounded by glowing textile art and living light, the class is guided by yoga teacher **Fabian Macklin**.  
Movement and breath unfold at a gentle pace, with attention on presence rather than performance.

The class is open to both beginners and experienced practitioners.  
No prior experience is required.

## Where is ANDETAG?

You’ll find ANDETAGin central mezzanine of the Hötorget Subway Station.

**From the subway:** Take the Kungsgatan escalator (middle of the platform), then the Kungsgatan exit towards Stureplan, and Sveavägen towards Sergels Torg.

**From the street:** Just below the stairs of the subway entrance on Kungsgatan 39.
