# Art Yoga at ANDETAG

## Yoga in Stockholm every Tuesday at 17:00

Art Yoga is a slow yoga class held inside Andetag’s immersive art environment in central Stockholm.  
A place to move, breathe, and settle, in the middle of the city.

Surrounded by glowing textile art and living light, the class is guided by yoga teacher **Fabian Macklin**.  
Movement and breath unfold at a gentle pace, with attention on presence rather than performance.

The class is open to both beginners and experienced practitioners.  
No prior experience is required.

[ Book Yoga ](https://andetag.understory.io/experience/cc2f4ed4e1709b93a454a1e5abad6595)

### Practical information

  * Time: Tuesdays at 17:00
  * Book your slot online before arriving
  * Location: Andetag, Stockholm
  * Doors close at 17:00. Please arrive on time.
  * You are welcome to arrive 20 minutes early and stay after the class.
  * Bring your own yoga mat if you wish. Practicing without a mat is also possible.
  * There are no changing rooms or showers. Lockers are available for personal belongings.



Art Yoga at Andetag is a quiet meeting between body and art.  
One shared breath each week.
