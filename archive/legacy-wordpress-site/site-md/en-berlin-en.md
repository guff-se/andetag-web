## A place to breathe

ANDETAG is a calm, light-based art museum where textile, light, and music move in sync with a shared breath.

The artworks are woven from optical fiber and traditional textile materials. Light travels inside the fabric itself, shifting slowly and never repeating. Original music guides the experience inward. The rhythm is based on a real breath, recorded during meditation.

It is not a spectacle. Not an event.  
It is a place to sit down, breathe, and stay for a while.

[ Visit ANdetag stockholm ](/en/stockholm)

## Already open in Stockholm

ANDETAG Stockholm opened in January 2025, in the heart of the city at Hötorget.

Since then, thousands of visitors have come to sit with the light, breathe along with the rhythm, and find a quiet pause inside the pace of the city.

> “The installation is incredibly immersive, with light, sound and textile art blending into a unique experience. It invites you to slow down, breathe and simply be present.”
> 
>  _— Edwin, visitor_

## The same breath, a new city

ANDETAG Berlin will be the second home of the breathing museum — the same concept, built at a larger scale.

Woven optical fiber textiles. Living light. Original music. A space built for stillness and presence, inside the rhythm of Berlin.

The works will breathe in sync with Stockholm and with ANDETAG artworks held by collectors around the world. A shared breath, connected across geography.

More details will follow as the opening approaches.
