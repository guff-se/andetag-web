# How to find ANDETAG

### In the middle of the city. Just below the surface.

ANDETAGis located in the **central entrance of Hötorget metro station** , in the heart of Stockholm. One step down from the pace of the city.

#### By metro

Take the metro to **Hötorget**.

Exit in the middle of the platform and follow signs for **Kungsgatan exit**.

Continue towards **Stureplan / Sveavägen mot Sergels torg**.

ANDETAGis located directly by the entrance.

#### From street level

The entrance is **just below the stairs** leading down to the metro at **Kungsgatan 39, Stockholm**

### Accessibility

Elevators are available both from the metro platform and at the entrance on Kungsgatan 39. The experience is wheelchair accessible.

[Read more about accessibility at ANDETAG](https://www.andetag.museum/en/stockholm/accessibility/)
