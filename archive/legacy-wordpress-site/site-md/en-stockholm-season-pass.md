# Season Pass for ANDETAG

### Return to your breathing

For some, ANDETAG becomes more than a single visit. It becomes a habit. A quiet return to the same space, the same light, the same slow rhythm.

The season pass is for those who want to make ANDETAG part of everyday life. A place to come back to, without planning. Without rushing.

Each visit is familiar, but never identical. Light and music move in sync with a shared breath, unfolding in subtle variations. You arrive when you need to. You stay as long as you wish.

### What the season pass includes

The season pass gives you **unlimited access for six months**.

You book time slots as usual, subject to availability, and can visit as often as you like during the period.

Some come once a week. Others more often. There is no right way.

### Price and conditions

**Season passes are purchased at the entrance.**

**Season pass: 950 SEK**  
Valid for 6 months from the date of purchase.

If you have already purchased a standard ticket, its value can be deducted when buying a season pass at the entrance. Simply show your ticket at the desk.

The season pass is personal and non-transferable.

### A quiet commitment

The season pass is not a promise of transformation. It is an invitation to continuity. A place that remains, ready when you are.

One breath. Again and again.
