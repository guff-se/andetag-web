# Date night at ANDETAG

## A quiet place to be together

ANDETAGis a still, immersive art experience in the heart of Stockholm.  
A place where two people can be close, be quiet, and share a moment without demands.  
The experience lasts about one hour and suits both first dates and couples.

You sit on cushions and blankets on the floor, surrounded by light, textile, and slow music.  
The gaze rests forward. The tempo softens.

There is nothing to perform.  
No pace to keep.  
Just time to be together.

ANDETAG is centrally located in Stockholm, with easy access to the metro and the movement of the city. It is easy to meet here after work or as a pause in the middle of the week.  
A place that requires no planning. Just presence.

### Practical information

Easily accessible by the Hötorget subway  
The experience lasts about one hour  
You sit, lie or cuddle with cushions and blankets on a soft carpet  
No alcohol is served  
Open year-round

Is ANDETAG suitable for a first date? 

Many people experience it that way. It is a calm environment where silence and presence are welcome.

Are you allowed to talk? 

In the final room, you are encouraged to be quiet together, but you may whisper to each other under the cover of the music.

Can you sit together? 

Yes. The final room is designed for sitting or lying together on the floor. Cushions and blankets are available to create your own quiet corner.

Is anything served? 

No, but you are welcome to bring your own water bottle.

Is it romantic? 

It is still. What it becomes is left open.
