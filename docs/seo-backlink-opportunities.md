## 20. Wikipedia — Stockholm museums list / Optical fibre textile

**Target search terms:** stockholm museums, list of museums in stockholm, optical fibre textile, breathing museum, andetag
**Difficulty:** 5/5
**Approach:** Wikipedia is the highest-authority backlink possible but has strict notability and conflict-of-interest policies. Two viable entry points: (1) **List of museums in Stockholm** — Andetag may qualify for inclusion if it meets Wikipedia's notability threshold (significant coverage in independent reliable sources). Do NOT create or edit the entry yourself — this violates COI policy. Instead, ensure sufficient independent press coverage exists (newspaper reviews, magazine features, tourism board mentions), then flag the omission on the article's Talk page for other editors to assess. (2) **Optical fibre textile** — if this material/technique is notable enough for a Wikipedia article or mention in an existing textile technology article, it could reference Andetag's installation. Again, this requires independent sourcing. Focus effort on generating the press coverage that makes Wikipedia inclusion natural rather than forcing the entry.
